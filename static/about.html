G|Days Kenya

About G|Days An insight into what the conference is about.

G|Days Kenya is a community-organized conference with the mission of building an ecosystem of top developers, businesses / decision makers and students in some of the most exciting emerging countries. The event this year is powered by the Google Developer Group and the Google Business Group.

About Google Developer Group (GDG)

Google Developer Groups (GDGs) are for developers who are interested in Google's developer technology; everything from the Android, App Engine, and Google Chrome platforms, to product APIs like the Maps API, YouTube API and Google Calendar API.

The GDG can take many forms — from just a few people getting together to watch the latest developer videos, to large gatherings with demos and tech talks, to events like code sprints and hackathons. However, at the core, GDGs are focused on developers and technical content, and the core audience are developers.

About GDG in Kenya

There are 8 GDG Chapters in Kenya GDG Nairobi, GDG Mombasa, GDG Kisumu, GDG Kimathi University, GDG Strathmore and GDG JKUAT, GDG Kabarak, GDG MMUST. The GDGs in Kenya is a vibrant community of Kenyan technologists and enthusiasts that actively employ Google technologies for work and pleasure. The community believes technology is an important key to development in the country and in Africa, and focuses on building local capacity to make that happen. It is the right place to hold technology-related discussions, learn and explore Google-related tools and technologies.

About Google Business Group (GBG)

GBGs are communities of business professionals that come together to share knowledge about Google web technologies for business success.

About GBG Kenya

The GBG Nairobi aims to bring together entrepreneurs, business executives, professionals, technology enthusiasts and anyone who is interested in learning more about what the Web offers to businesses.

We meet and learn from fellow members (experienced professionals), passionate to help entrepreneurs transform their business with web technology. This is an initiative managed by leaders and volunteers independent of Google. Join GBG Kenya on G+.
